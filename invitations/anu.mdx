As you've [touched on](https://www.workingtheorys.com/p/stability), stability is disappearing[^powerlaw]. Despite being easier than ever to create, it's becoming more difficult to stand out and actually *create value*. The creative path has become riskier.

By letting us share risk & reward in the journey itself, personal tokens help us withstand greater risk and pursue more artistic and ambitious lives.

I think you would thrive operating a personal token for yourself. You could incentivize collaboration and capital to help you across building, writing, investing. I also see you becoming a great investor in personal tokens. I have a feeling this will come more naturally to you than investing in companies.

Personal tokens won't work if they are restricted to a crypto / tech audience. It must be designed and presented in a way that enables artists broadly, no matter the medium (code, business, music, film, etc.). You have the ability to perceive the personal token in this broader context in ways that few others can. I would love your feedback.

[^powerlaw]: I believe stability will continue to disappear. AI pushes us towards more complex, creative pursuits by doing the less creative work for us. And the more complex / creative a task, the risker it becomes (i.e, more extreme power law distribution of outcomes). Sharing risk & reward in life itself will become much more common.
